Know little more about us..


We Ag Fund act as a common platform for resolving agronomist problem in their everyday farmland. Apart from analysing the soil type and giving the right farming plan for a season we also provide fellow citizens with an opportunity to help the farmers by funding them to clear their debts. Through us, you can check the regular hazards caused in farmlands of your locality and preventive measure for legitimate administration of the development land. Our vision is to prevent agronomist suicide and provide good organic yield.
